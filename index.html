About Me

Ashim Panthi is an Engineer-in-Training (EIT) with a Bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering from Tribhuvan University. His interests focus on structural analysis, seismic engineering, and the design of earthquake-resistant infrastructure. Through academic research and project work, he has developed experience in structural modeling and finite element analysis using tools such as SAP2000, ETABS, AutoCAD, MATLAB, and GIS platforms. Ashim is passionate about designing resilient structures and contributing to the development of safe infrastructure in earthquake-prone regions.
