About Me

I am an Engineer-in-Training (EIT) with a Bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering from Tribhuvan University. My interests lie in structural analysis, seismic engineering, and the design of earthquake-resistant infrastructure. Through academic projects and technical training, I have developed experience in structural modeling and finite element analysis using engineering tools such as SAP2000, ETABS, AutoCAD, MATLAB, and GIS platforms. I am passionate about applying engineering principles to design safe, resilient structures and contributing to the development of sustainable infrastructure in earthquake-prone regions.
