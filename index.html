Academic Research

Design Demands of RC Buildings Due to Irregularities

• Conducted a comparative seismic performance analysis of RC-framed buildings with torsional, stiffness, and diaphragm irregularities against regular structures, in compliance with Nepal National Building Code (NBC 105:2020).
• Utilized Finite Element modeling in SAP2000 to evaluate storey drift, internal forces, and lateral seismic demands under different irregularity scenarios.
•Assessed the impact of structural irregularities on design requirements, including section sizing and reinforcement detailing..
• Provided insights into how architectural and functional constraints influencing irregularities can significantly affect structural design demand in seismic-resistant RC buildings.
